## Welcome to Goobercraft!

Welcome to the official Goobercraft documentation! This site contains everything you need to know about our Minecraft community, including server information, rules, guidelines, and more.

## What is Goobercraft?

Goobercraft is a Minecraft community dedicated to providing a fun, friendly, and inclusive environment for players of all skill levels. Whether you're a veteran builder or just starting your Minecraft journey, you'll find a home here.

If you need assistance or have questions:
