## Books

### Waystone Book

This recipe crafts an empty teleportation book that can be used to teleport to any waystone after it's been used on the target waystone.

![Waystone Book crafting recipe with 4 Ender Pearls and 1 Book](/assets/images/TeleportBook.png)

- Materials Required
  - 4 Ender Pearl
  - 1 Book

### Death Book

This recipe crafts a book that will teleport you to your last death location. **Use wisely though, if you died in lava or the void, this may not be the place you want to return to.**

![Death Book crafting recipe with 3 Ender Pearls, 1 Book, and 1 Totem of Undying](/assets/images/DeathBook1.png)

- Materials Required
  - 3 Ender Peral
  - 1 Book
  - 1 Totem of Undying

## Mailbox

This recipe crafts a mailbox for use with our UltraMailBox Plugin

![Mailbox crafting recipe with Iron Ingot, Book and Quill, Paper, and Oak Fence](/assets/images/Mailbox.png)

- Materials Required
  - 1 Iron Ingot
  - 1 Book and Quill
  - 1 Paper
  - 1 Oak Fence

## Waystones

_**Both Public and Private Waystones are limited to 5 placements per waystone type**_

### Public Waystone

Both of these recipes craft a waystone that can be used by everyone

1) ![Public Waystone crafting recipe with Gold Block](/assets/images/WaystonePublic.png) 2) ![Public Waystone crafting recipe with Redstone Block](/assets/images/WaystonePublic2.png)

- Materials Required
  - 2 Smooth Stone Slab
  - 1 Polished Andesite
  - 1 Gold Block _**OR**_ 1 Redstone Block
  - 1 Ender Chest

### Private Waystone

This recipe crafts a waystone that can be controlled by an access list

![Private Waystone crafting recipe with Name Tags and Emerald Block](/assets/images/WaystonePrivate.png)

- Materials Required
  - 2 Smooth Stone Slab
  - 1 Polished Andesite
  - 2 Name Tags
  - 1 Emerald Block
  - 3 Ender Chest
