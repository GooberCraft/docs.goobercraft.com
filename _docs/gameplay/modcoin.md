## How do I earn a modcoin?

- Participating in Community Week by working on that month's eligible task(s) for a minimum of 1 hour. You can earn 1 coin per community week.
- Lighting up the caves under your overworld base will reward you with 1 modcoin. If you are sharing a base with someone then you both we be rewarded with 1 modcoin each. To redeem these you will just fill out the [community event form](https://forms.gle/3KzRc3Me5gWeaxh16)

## Redeems

Item Redeems

- 1 Armor Trim of your choice
- 1 Budding Amethyst
- 1 Elytra (Unenchanted)
- 1 Enchanted Golden Apple
- 1 Heart of the Sea
- 1 Knockback Stick (Knockback level: 10)
- 1 Music Disc of your choice (Does not include custom music discs)
- 1 Nether Star
- Access to the Head Database system
- 1 (24) hour non kick timer pass to be used to afk at a farm - _**Can not separate time must be used in one 24 hour period.**_
- 64 Suspicious Sand or Suspicious Gravel (non-lootable, decorative only)
- 1 Mod-Max Potion - 1 4-hour duration potion from the list below:

    1. Super Fast Dig Mode - a potion that grants 4 hours of **haste**
    2. Speed
    3. Jump Boost
    4. Regeneration
    5. Resistance
    6. Fire Resistance
    7. Water Breathing
    8. Night Vision
    9. Glowing
    10. Luck
    11. Slow Falling
    12. Conduit Power
    13. Dolphin's Grace

    **Please note: This redeem is not refundable or replaceable upon loss of effect. Dying or drinking milk will clear these effects, please use responsibly! These potions cannot be crafted into splash potions.**

**Mob Redeems**

- Locator: Slime Chunk - Locate 1 slime chunk within a general specified area
- Locator: Mob Spawner - Locate 1 specified mob spawner within your claim
- 2 Overworld Trading Piglins - Spawn in 2 Piglins that are immune to zombification to the overworld. Maximum 2 per base. - _Beware Iron golems can kill them and we will not replace piglins unless a mod coin is used again._
- 2 Vanilla Mobs - Spawn any two vanilla mobs of your choice. They can be a specific variant (blue axolotl, brown panda, etc.) and do not have to be the same 2 mobs. There are a few exceptions with this redeem:
  1. Withers and Ender Dragons are excluded from this redeem. Villagers are allowed to be purchased with modcoins, but they have their own redeem option listed in a lower section.
  2. Wardens are 1 modcoin each.
- 1 Villager - Can be spawned at a location of your choice. _**Please note: If this villager dies or wonders off he can not be replaced. Would need to be repurchased with a mod coin.**_
