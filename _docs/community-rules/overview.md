## Community Rules

Our community rules are designed to ensure everyone has a positive and enjoyable experience on Goobercraft. All players are expected to follow these rules at all times.

## Core Principles

1. **Respect** - Treat all players, staff, and community members with respect
2. **Integrity** - Play fairly and honestly
3. **Inclusivity** - Welcome and support all community members
4. **Responsibility** - Take ownership of your actions

## Rule Categories

### Behavioral Rules

- No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- No bullying or personal attacks
- Keep chat appropriate for all ages
- Respect staff decisions

### Gameplay Rules

- No cheating, hacking, or exploiting
- No griefing or stealing from other players
- Respect property boundaries
- Follow server-specific game rules

### Communication Rules

- Use appropriate language
- No spamming or excessive caps
- No advertising other servers
- Keep discussions civil and constructive

## Enforcement

Rules are enforced by our moderation team. Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

See [Consequences](/docs/community-rules/consequences/) for more details on rule enforcement.

## Questions?

If you're unsure about whether something is allowed, ask a staff member before proceeding. It's always better to ask first!
