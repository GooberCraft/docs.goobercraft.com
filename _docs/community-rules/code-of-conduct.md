## Code of Conduct

This code of conduct applies to all Goobercraft community spaces, including the Minecraft server, Discord, forums, and any other official communication channels.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- **Be Respectful**: Value each other's ideas, styles, and viewpoints
- **Be Helpful**: Assist new players and contribute positively to the community
- **Be Patient**: Remember that everyone starts somewhere and learns at different paces
- **Be Constructive**: Offer helpful criticism and accept it gracefully
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and help them feel at home
- **Communicate Clearly**: Be clear in your intentions and respectful in your disagreements

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

- **Harassment**: Including offensive comments, intimidation, stalking, or unwanted attention
- **Discrimination**: Based on any protected characteristic
- **Trolling**: Deliberately inflammatory or off-topic messages
- **Doxxing**: Publishing others' private information without permission
- **Sexual Content**: Unwelcome sexual attention or imagery
- **Threats**: Of violence or harm towards any individual or group
- **Impersonation**: Pretending to be another player or staff member

## In-Game Conduct

### Building and Griefing

- Build respectfully and consider the impact on others
- Do not destroy, damage, or modify other players' builds without permission
- Do not steal items from other players
- Use common areas respectfully

### PvP and Combat

- Follow server rules regarding PvP (varies by server type)
- No spawn camping or unfair combat tactics
- Respect players who don't want to engage in PvP

### Chat and Communication

- Keep chat family-friendly
- No excessive caps, spam, or flooding
- No advertising other servers or services
- Use appropriate channels for different types of discussion

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Screenshot Evidence**: Capture screenshots when possible
2. **Contact Staff**: Reach out to moderators or administrators
3. **Be Specific**: Provide details about what happened, when, and who was involved
4. **Be Patient**: Staff will investigate and take appropriate action

## Staff Responsibilities

Staff members are responsible for:

- Enforcing this code of conduct fairly and consistently
- Taking appropriate action in response to violations
- Maintaining confidentiality when handling reports
- Leading by example in all community interactions

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all Goobercraft spaces and when an individual is representing the community in public spaces.

## Updates

This code of conduct may be updated from time to time. Community members will be notified of significant changes.
