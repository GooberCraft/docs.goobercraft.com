## Welcome to GooberCraft Season 2

Welcome, Goobers! GooberCraft Season 2 is a modded Forge Minecraft server where we build, explore, and thrive together. This documentation site is your one-stop resource for everything you need to knowâ€”server rules, mod info, helpful commands, how-tos, and onboarding details for new players.

### What You'll Find Here

- **Getting Started**: Client setup and connection guides
- **Server Rules**: Community standards and content creation guidelines
- **Server Information**: Complete mod list, plugins, and technical details
- **Gameplay Features**: ModCoin economy, shopping district, custom recipes, and commands
- **Plugins & Tools**: Status indicators, villager optimization, and more
- **FAQ**: Common questions and voice chat setup

### Getting Started

1. Set up your [CurseForge Client](/docs/client-setup/) with our modpack
2. Review our [Server Rules](/docs/server-rules/overview/)
3. Check out [Server Information](/docs/server-info/overview/) to learn about mods and features
4. Join our Discord and start playing!
