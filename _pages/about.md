## About Goobercraft

Goobercraft is a Minecraft community dedicated to providing a welcoming, fun, and inclusive environment for players of all skill levels and backgrounds.

## Our Mission

We strive to create a Minecraft server where:

- **Everyone feels welcome** regardless of experience level
- **Creativity thrives** through collaborative and individual projects
- **Fair play** is expected and maintained
- **Community comes first** with active engagement and events
- **Respect** is the foundation of all interactions

## Our Values

### Inclusivity

We welcome players from all backgrounds, ages (within Minecraft's EULA), and skill levels. Whether you're a redstone genius or just learning to craft your first pickaxe, you have a place here.

### Integrity

We believe in honest gameplay, fair administration, and transparent communication. Our staff are held to high standards and our rules apply equally to everyone.

### Community

Goobercraft isn't just a server - it's a community. We encourage collaboration, helping others, and building friendships that extend beyond the game.

### Quality

We maintain high-quality server infrastructure, active moderation, and regularly updated features to ensure the best possible experience.
